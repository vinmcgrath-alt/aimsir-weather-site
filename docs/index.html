Aimsir: Your Irish Weather Assistant

Ireland is known as the Emerald Isle, and part of that is due to our unique climate. Sitting on the edge of Europe, surrounded by water, the Atlantic Ocean and its prevailing winds shape our weather. That means three things:

  1. We have a relatively mild climate (it's rarely extreme!)
  2. It can change quickly (four seasons in a day!)
  3. It can rain — a lot (bring a coat or an umbrella!)

So in many ways, it's a great climate for getting out and exploring — you just need to know how to do that.

That’s why we created Aimsir — to provide specific, localised weather updates and suggested activities for both locals and visitors. You can also tailor the experience to your own preferences for more relevant results.
