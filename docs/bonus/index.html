Irish Weather

People have lived in Ireland since the end of the last Ice Age, and through all that time our weather has remained a reliable conversation starter. Here at Aimsir, we aim to give you the information you need to plan your trips and activities with confidence. Ireland experiences over 150 days of rain per year (though rarely all day). The warmest month is typically July, while January is usually the coldest. The west coast receives the most rainfall, and the south‑east the least. Snow is uncommon, but can occur in higher elevations — though you won’t need skis.
